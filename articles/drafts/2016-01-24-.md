Simplified big data can be stated as much of anything. But what is “much” in this context and what means “anything”?

In most application big data is gathered by recoring a lot of data, for example amazon tracks nearly everything when people shop at thier website. It does not matter for amazon at this time to have is any use for it at the time. This tracking embrace very different types of data, for example the courser movment can be considered as a curve while the clicking behavior can be understood as a nominal type of data.
